Kaun Sa ITR Form, Kisko Pata Hai? Aarambha Ko Pata Hai!

Do you have income from house property? Are you running a small business or profession and opting for the presumptive taxation scheme? The nuances are many, and getting it wrong can lead to headaches later.

With Karta, you don’t need to be a tax expert. Our system, leveraging advanced AI, asks you simple, natural language questions. Form-16 Se Voice Deductions Tak: The Magic of Automation

The heart of ITR filing for salaried individuals is often the Form-16. It’s a treasure trove of information, but manually inputting all those fields can be tedious and prone to errors. This is where Karta truly shines.

Form-16: Jhatpat Auto-Detection

You simply upload your Form-16 (or even just snap a picture!), and Karta’s AI instantly reads and understands every detail. Salary, TDS, allowances – all those complex numbers are automatically detected and populated into the correct fields. Imagine the time saved! No more squinting at small print, no more accidental typos. It’s precise, it’s fast, and it’s incredibly accurate.

Voice Deductions: Bolo Aur Ho Jaayega!

Now, here’s where the real game-changer comes in. The deductions. This is where most Indians get stuck, either missing out on legitimate savings or getting confused by the various sections. With Karta, you don’t need to remember section numbers or fill out complicated forms. You just speak. Seriously.

Or, "Medical insurance ke liye 25 hazaar," and it correctly maps it to Section 80D. It’s conversational, it’s intuitive, and it’s designed for the way real Indians talk about their finances.

  • HRA (House Rent Allowance): Just tell Karta your rent paid, and it calculates your HRA exemption. No need for complex formulas.
  • 80C Investments: From PPF to ELSS, life insurance premiums to home loan principal repayment – simply state your investments, and Karta takes care of the rest.

Beyond the Salaried: Handling Edge Cases with Ease

While a large chunk of our audience comprises salaried individuals, we know that the Indian economy is dynamic. More and more people are freelancing, investing in the stock market, or juggling multiple income streams. Karta is built to handle these complexities too, without adding to your stress.

Freelancers Aur Business Professionals: Presumptive Taxation Made Easy

If you're a freelancer, consultant, or run a small business, you might be eligible for presumptive taxation under sections 44AD, 44ADA, or 44AE. These sections simplify tax filing by allowing you to declare a certain percentage of your gross receipts as profit, avoiding the need for detailed bookkeeping. Karta guides you through this, asking simple questions about your income and automatically calculating your taxable profit. No need to hire an accountant just for this!

Capital Gains: Shares, Property, aur Mutual Funds

Investing in the stock market or property is becoming increasingly common. But calculating capital gains (both short-term and long-term) can be a nightmare. Selling shares, mutual funds, or even property involves understanding holding periods, indexation benefits, and various tax rates. With Karta, you can input your transaction details, and our system intelligently calculates your capital gains and losses, ensuring you report them accurately and claim any permissible exemptions.

Multiple Employers: No More Juggling Form-16s

Did you switch jobs during the financial year? Many Indians do. This often means receiving multiple Form-16s, each with different TDS deductions and salary components. Consolidating this information correctly for ITR filing can be a major headache. Karta allows you to upload multiple Form-16s, and our AI seamlessly merges the data, ensuring all your income and TDS credits are accurately accounted for. No more manual calculations or fear of double taxation.
