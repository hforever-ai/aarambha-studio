22/7 — The Woodcutter Principle

Do lakkadhaare the. Competition tha.
Pehle ne kaha: 24 ghante kaam karunga. Nonstop.
Doosre ne kaha: Main 22 ghante kaam karunga. 2 ghante kulhaadi tej karunga.

Din bhar kaam kiya.
22 ghante wale ne zyada lakdi kaati.

Kyunki tez kulhaadi — thodi mehnat mein zyada karta hai.
⏸️
Pause to Sharpen

Stop. Reflect. Rebuild.

No feature ships until it's right. We'd rather delay than ship broken.

🎯
Optimize First

Don't scale broken things.

Fix the core until it's perfect, then 10x it. Scale amplifies flaws.

🔥
Build Right

Ship once, correctly.

Not ten times, broken. Quality compounds — so does technical debt.

Applied to Shrutam

Shrutam ki ek ek cheez soch ke bani hai. "Mother tongue first" — ek feature nahi, ek decision tha. Blind mode — 50 lakh students ke liye, revenue zero. Kyunki yahi sahi tha.

Positioning

Bharat First — Not India First.

"India" that gets funded: metro cities, English speakers. "Bharat" that needs AI: villages, Hindi speakers, no tutor, tight budget. Aarambha builds for Bharat.

  • 🏘️
    600,000+ villages with internet

    Infrastructure is there. Native AI isn't. We close that gap.

  • 🗣️
    5 languages — native, not translated

    SAAVI thinks in Hindi. The model wasn't trained on English and retrofitted. That's the difference.

  • ♿
    Blind mode — built in, not bolted on

    50 lakh visually-impaired students. Zero other edtech serves them. Shrutam does — free, forever.
